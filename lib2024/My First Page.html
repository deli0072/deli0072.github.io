My favourite movie: Rose Red

A group of people with psychic powers are invited to spend the night in a haunted house. Dr. Joyce Reardon, psychology professor, leads a team of psychics into the decrepit mansion known as Rose Red. Her efforts unleash the spirit of former owner Ellen Rimbauer and uncover the horrifying secrets of those who lived and died there.
